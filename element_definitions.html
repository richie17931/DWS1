Element Definitions

Headings
Summary of the information in the body.
Paragraphs
A few sentences pertaining to the same topic grouped together.
Bold
Thicker text to highlight keywords.
Italic
Slanted letters to put emphasis on words and make them stand out.
Superscript
Suffixes of dates or mathematical concepts.
Subscript
Footnotes or chemical formulas that use small lowered letters.
Line Breaks
Adds a new line.
Horizontal Rule
Creates a break between themes or topics.
Strong
Indicates strong importance.
Emphasis
Subtly changes the meaning of a sentence.
Blockquote
Long quotes that take a paragraph.
Quotes
Shorter quotes within a paragraph.
Abbreviation
Shortens text.
Cite
Referencing material.
Definition
Defines a term.
Address
Contains information on the author of the page.
Insert
Shows inserted content.
Delete
Shows deleted content.
Strikethrough
Shows something is no longer relevant.
Ordered List
Numbered list where order matters.
List Item
An item in the list
Unordered List
Items listed with bullet points.
Definition Lists
A series of terms and definitions.
Definition Term
The term to be defined.
Defined Definition
The definition of the term.
